# AI Safety & LLM Behavior Experiments

This repository contains my ongoing research on the behavior, safety, and reliability of large language models in real-world applications.

## Research focus

- Behavior under edge-case and adversarial prompts  
- Model consistency and reliability  
- Failure mode identification  
- Safety and alignment in production systems  

## Methodology

- Structured prompt testing  
- Batch experiments and logging  
- Comparative response analysis  

## Context

I am an independent researcher with a background in building AI-powered tools, including automation systems and Telegram bots.

My goal is to better understand how language models behave outside of controlled environments and identify potential safety risks.

## Status

This is an active research repository. Experiments and findings will be added progressively.
